Nissan 370z

The successor of the 350z and the latest generation of Z's

by Andrew Orcilla

Nissan 370z Information

The Nissan 370Z (known as the Fairlady Z Z34 in Japan) is a 2-door, 2-seater sports car (S-segment in Europe) manufactured by Nissan Motor Company.It was announced on October 29, 2006 and was first shown at an event in Los Angeles ahead of the 2008 Greater LA Auto Show, before being officially unveiled at the show itself. The 370Z is the sixth-generation of the Nissan Z-car line, succeeding the 350Z. The 370z was released in 2009 till today and is know for being one of the best sport cars to own without breaking your bank account.

Motor information

The 370z has upgraded from the previous 350z motor being a 3.5 L VQ35DE/HR to a 3.7 L VQ37HR. Now with a higher rev limit it produces a powerful ~334 horsepower at 7000rpm. This 370z also has a high performance model called the Nismo 370z. This model has better intakes and a header back exhuast nismo system, also a custom tune for maximum power. This specific trim produces 350 horsepower at 7,400rpm.
