Nissan Z

The Nissan Z is a sports car that is manufactured by Nissan Moters Ltd. This specific model has been in production since 1969 and has gone through six generations.

This Website is more educational purposes only. I describe the evolution in car generations that the nissan model "z" goes throught the year.

All information was sourced from Wikipedia and added info by me (Andrew Orcilla)

My Interest on the Topic

I started growing an interest in cars when i saw my first Nissan GTR. The first thing that came to my mind is that it was such a cool looking car so I did my research. Finding out that the car cost $60,000 and more I went to look and see if Nissan had any more models of sport cars. Soon enough I ran into the 350Z, one that I can actually afford.
I started to to research in finding the strength and weaknesses of the car, and to my surprise many people say the quickness and the engineering of the car make it a good bang for your buck. It was 3 years later from the time i started doing research is when i bought my first Nissan 350Z I fell in love, knowing that there was a later model (the 370z) i still preffered the look of the predeccesor. Sadly the dream fell quick and a close friend totaled the car. I do plan on getting another one but later in the future. For now i still do research learning more and more about my favorite car.
