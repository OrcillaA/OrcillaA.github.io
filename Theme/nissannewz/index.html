The Future of Z's

by Andrew Orcilla

Rumors and Concepts

At the beginning September 2018 rumors have been spreading that there will be a new Nissan Z in production. Many are predicting they will reveal the new generation at the Tokyo Motor Show which is scheduled to happen October 2019. Apperently the play is to team up with Mercedes and bring in a new concept to how the engine is built. It will replace the original 3.7 V6 with 4 cylinder engine that will possibly have two sets of turbos.

Modifications and Custom work

Many individuals who own a Z often times modify their car. Whether it's for style or performance each one has a unique sense of taste towards the owner. I myself have owned a 2008 350z and I can say it was an experience to have. The interior feels like a rocketship with every display turned towards the driver geared to give that experience where you have a personal connection and control of the car.

Even though it sounds like a close to perfect car everything has its flaws. The Z's are know to have a lot of road noise when when taking it for a drive. Also the interior material is made out of a cheap plastic that scratches easily. Most Z's that you find "used" will most likely have interior mark ups.
